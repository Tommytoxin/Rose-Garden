ABOUT US

The Rose Garden was Founded by Ryno Van Niekerk and Mariaan van Niekerk in 1994.
This was a purchase from Ryno to his wife as it was her dream to be a florist.
Now more than 20 years later the store has been passed down to their oldest daughter, Bianca Van Niekerk.
At the Rose Garden, we carefully tailor our floral bouquets to our clients vision and need.
Whether it be for elaborate events, a get-well-soon gift or a simple I-Love-You, the Rose Garden has you covered.
